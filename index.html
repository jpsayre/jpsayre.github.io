I'm Jeff Sayre — an engineer and data scientist with 15+ years of experience spanning mechanical design, electrical systems integration, and data analytics. I spent most of my career at Caterpillar, where I progressed from designing hydraulic systems for large mining equipment to leading telematics deployments and building data quality and analytics tools for customer fleets.

I hold 5 U.S. patents and have led teams across hydraulic, electrical, and telematics disciplines. More recently, I've focused on applied data science — building predictive models, data pipelines, and full-stack web applications. My side project, Solar Intel, is a lead-generation platform that predicts residential solar adoption using machine learning.
